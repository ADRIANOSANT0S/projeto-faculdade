Public Communications

Bancaspa - BANCO MÚLTIPLO ("BCP") informs that unscrupulous individuals may be using the company's name, brand, logo, registration information, as well as BCP's CNPJ, to advertise fraudulent offers of credit, banking services, and/or loans for individuals and companies under the name of BCP on fake electronic domains.

In light of these events, BCP informs that it does not use third-party services such as bank correspondents, investment advisors (formerly Autonomous Investment Agent - AAI), etc., and neither does BCP use blogs, social networks, or similar platforms to promote any product from its areas of operation.

Therefore, we request that you remain vigilant and suspicious of any contact made in the name of BCP with the intention of offering loans or providing any banking services, especially involving the provision of information, deposits into accounts, or payment of bank slips. This is not how BCP operates with its clients, and you may be a victim of fraud.

We are available for clarifications through our Official Contact Channels or through our Ombudsman Channel: br.ouvidoria@bancaspa.com.
