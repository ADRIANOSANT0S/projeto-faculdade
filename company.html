Fund Statement Information

Broker Bank S.A. – Banco Múltiplo, is a private investment bank belonging to the CAFX Group, alongside Intesa Broker, one of the main banking groups in Europe.

Who we are


Broker Bank S.A. - Banco Múltiplo is the only digital bank with a direct retail operational presence capable of providing the best financial solutions for companies, financial institutions and public organizations. The Bank is licensed to operate as a Multiple Bank in the EU (EUROS) and in the local Italian foreign exchange market.
